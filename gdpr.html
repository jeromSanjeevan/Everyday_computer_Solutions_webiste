GDPR

Policy statement

We are Everyday Computer Solutions Ltd. During a contract period with school, we will use information that we gather in relation to them for various purpose. Information that we hold in relation to an individual in known as their “personal data”. This will include data that we obtain from the individual directly and data about the individual that we obtain from other people and resources. We might also need to continue to hold an individual’s data for a period of time after terminating the contract and not keeping it for longer than is necessary. Anything that we do with an individual’s personal data is known as “processing” This document sets out what personal data we will hold about our customers, why we process that data, where do we get information, why do we use this information and with Whom is the information shared, and the right of individuals in relation to their personal data processed by us.

What Personal Information do we process?

We will collect and hold the following information

  • Customer and Suppliers
  • Who you work for, Job Title or department
  • Email address, Phone numbers depending on the nature of the relationship with Customer/suppliers.

Where do we get information?

A lot of the information we have about our customers from the individuals Directly. However, we may also obtain information from websites.

Why do we use this information?

  • To help us identify our customers.
  • To provide customer care;
  • To carry out marketing and sales;
  • To communicate with customers
  • To provide you with information, products or services that you request from us or which we feel may interest you, where you have consented to be contacted for such purposes.

With whom is the information shared?

We may share your personal data on to third-part service providers contracted to Everyday Computer Solutions in the course of dealing with you to purchase goods and services from third-party behalf of the customers. In situation, we will share your personal data with your consent.

Changes to our policy

We will occasionally update this Privacy Policy statement, we encourage you to frequently check the statement.
